About Weather App

This application helps users get real-time weather updates and forecast data based on their location. It is designed to help users especially village health teams to acertain the weather of the area so they can plan better. It is part of a class final project to showcase how APIs can be used to retrieve weather information.

DIY - Enable Geolocation

To use the weather guide app efficiently, ensure your browser has access to your location:

  • Go to your browser settings.
  • Find the "Location" section under privacy or security settings -> permissions.
  • Type in the site url, then open its permissions and allow this website to access your location.
  • Reload the page and press "Find" to get the weather for your area.
  • Alternatively.
  • Click on the "Find" button on the site. The site will prompt you to allow it to access your location. Click "Allow", then it will upload the information for you.
